External Muun Server

Powered by our friends at Muun.

Muun is a highly optimized fork of Node Unblocker that lets you browse and play games with fewer blocks and better performance. Enter a URL below to start browsing through the external server.

You can:

  • Play blocked IO games with minimal performance loss.
  • Load webpages that require heavy scripting.
  • Sign in to select websites.
  • Use apps that depend on WebSocket connections.
